MoSe2


MoSe2 is a kind of TMDs

In monolayer MoSe2, the main optical response originats from exciton and exciton complexes. The excitons shows valley dependent optical response. The right and left circularly polarized light selectively couples to excitons in different valleys.

The interaction strength of intervalley exciton-electron interaction is greater than the intravalley one. When applying a magnetic field, the degeneracy between K and K' is lifted, making the electrons spin polarized when the monolayer is slightly doped. In this situation,the optic response of right and left circular polarized light shows significant difference[ref]. This kind of response enables the detection of electron polarization in MoSe2.

Wigner Crystal

Monolayer and bilayer MoSe2 can hold Wigner crystal when properly doped. The detection method is mainly absorption. The Umklapp absorption peak is the mark of the formation of additional periodic potential, and thus the Wigner crystal . The Umklapp signal disappers when the doping density is high[ref]. Also, the intermediate state between solid and fermion liquid was observed using the same technique, but in a 16 mK dilution refrigerator and a low excitation light power below 0.7 nW[ref ].
