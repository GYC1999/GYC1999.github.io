MoSe2


MoSe2 is a kind of TMDs

Wigner Crystal

Monolayer and bilayer MoSe2 can hold Wigner crystal when properly doped. The detection method is mainly absorption. The Umklapp absorption peak is the mark of the formation of additional periodic potential, and thus the Wigner crystal [ref]. Also, the intermediate state between solid and fermion liquid was observed using the same technique[ref].
